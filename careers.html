How it works

How do I apply?

Email hr@velocube.net with the role name in the subject line. Include a short introduction, your experience, and a resume if you have one.

We reply to every application, usually within a day. If it looks like a fit, we'll set up a short call before making a decision.

Is this remote?

Yes. Every open role is fully remote with flexible hours, so you can work from wherever fits your life.

Do I need prior experience?

It depends on the role. Marketing and Support benefit from relevant experience, but Brand Ambassadors need no technical or industry background, we train you.
