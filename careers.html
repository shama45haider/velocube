Careers

Join the team

Velocube is growing. We're hiring for three roles. Read the descriptions below and apply by email.

Marketing Team

Marketing

You promote Velocube and help bring in new clients. You plan campaigns, create content, and track what works.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and run marketing campaigns
  • Create content for social media and email
  • Track results and report what's working
  • Research local businesses that need our services

Requirements

  • Marketing experience or a strong portfolio
  • Clear writing skills
  • Comfortable with major social platforms
  • Organized and self-directed

Growth: Strong performers take ownership of full campaigns and can grow into a senior marketing role as the company expands.

Customer Support

Support

You're the first person clients talk to. You answer questions, track requests, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

Responsibilities

  • Respond to client questions by email and phone
  • Track requests and follow up until they're resolved
  • Help new clients get started
  • Pass technical issues to the development team clearly

Requirements

  • Clear, friendly communication in writing and on calls
  • Organized — nothing gets forgotten
  • Basic familiarity with how websites work
  • Patience and a calm approach to problems

Growth: Support team members learn every part of the business and can move into project coordination or account management.

Brand Ambassadors

Outreach

You represent Velocube in your network and community. You connect local businesses that need websites with our team.

Responsibilities

  • Introduce Velocube to businesses in your network
  • Share our work and content online
  • Refer potential clients to the team
  • Represent the brand professionally

Requirements

  • Outgoing and comfortable starting conversations
  • An active local or online network
  • Professional and reliable
  • No technical experience needed

Growth: Commission-based, with higher rates as you bring in more clients. Top ambassadors can move into full marketing roles.

How to apply

Email hr@velocube.net with the role name in the subject line. Include a short introduction, your experience, and a resume if you have one. We reply to every application.

Want to work with us?

Send your application to hr@velocube.net with the role name in the subject line.
