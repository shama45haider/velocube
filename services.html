Services

What we build and manage

Nineteen services, one company. Every project is custom. If you need something that isn't listed here, ask — the answer is usually yes.

Development

01

Business Websites

A custom website designed and built for your business. Includes mobile-friendly layouts, fast load times, contact forms, and search engine basics set up from day one. Your website is usually the first thing customers check — it should look professional and work everywhere.

02

Mobile App Development

Apps for iOS and Android, built for your customers or your internal team. Includes design, development, and help publishing to the app stores. Useful when your business needs features a website can't offer, like push notifications or offline access.

03

Custom Dashboard Panels

A private admin panel where you manage your website's content, orders, customers, and data in one place. Built around how your business actually works. You make changes yourself without waiting on a developer.

04

Customer Portals

Secure login areas where your customers view their accounts, orders, documents, or bookings. Includes account creation, password recovery, and permission controls. Cuts down repeat questions because clients can find their own information.

05

SQL Database Setup

A properly structured database for your website or app. Includes design, setup, security, and backups. A well-built database keeps your data fast, organized, and safe as your business grows.

06

API Integrations

Connections between your website and the tools you already use — payment processors, booking systems, shipping carriers, CRMs, and more. Data moves automatically instead of being copied by hand.

07

AI Integrations

AI features added to your website or app. Common examples: a chatbot trained on your business that answers customer questions 24/7, automated content tools, and smart search. Built and tested so they help customers instead of frustrating them.

Infrastructure

08

Domain Registration & Configuration

We register your domain name and handle the technical setup — DNS records, redirects, and SSL certificates. You own the domain. We make sure it points to the right place and stays secure.

09

Business Email Setup

Professional email addresses on your own domain, like name@yourbusiness.com. Includes account setup, spam protection, and configuration on your devices. A branded email address looks more credible than a free one.

10

Website Hosting

Fast, secure hosting for your website. Includes SSL, backups, and uptime monitoring. We manage the servers so you never have to think about them.

11

Security Improvements

Protection for your website and customer data. Includes SSL setup, secure logins, software updates, and fixes for common vulnerabilities. Prevents the kind of problems that are expensive to clean up later.

12

Performance Optimization

We make slow websites fast. Includes image compression, code cleanup, caching, and Core Web Vitals improvements. Faster sites keep more visitors and rank better on Google.

Brand & Design

13

Logo Design

A custom logo designed for your business, delivered in every format you need — web, print, and social media. Includes revisions until it's right.

14

Branding

A complete visual identity: colors, fonts, logo usage, and design rules. Everything your business puts out — website, cards, social posts — looks like it comes from the same company.

15

Custom Graphics

Graphics made for your business: social media posts, banners, menus, flyers, and website imagery. Designed to match your brand instead of looking like a template.

Growth & Support

16

SEO Optimization

We optimize your website to rank on Google, Bing, and Yahoo. Includes keyword research, on-page optimization, technical fixes, local SEO, and structured data. Available as a one-time project or monthly service.

17

Google Analytics & Search Console Setup

We connect your website to Google's free measurement tools and configure them correctly. You see who visits your site, what they do, and which searches bring them in. Includes conversion tracking so you know what's working.

18

Website Maintenance

Ongoing updates, backups, bug fixes, and monitoring. Available as a monthly plan — see the management page for details. Your website stays current without you touching it.

19

Technical Consulting

Straight answers about your website, tools, or technical decisions. Useful before a big purchase, a redesign, or when something isn't working and you want a second opinion. No jargon, no upsell.
